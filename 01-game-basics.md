# Game Basics

This document describes the fundamental mechanics shared by all heroes and game modes in Bullet Echo and Bullet Echo India.

---

# Hero Structure

Every hero has the following abilities and progression.

## Main Ability

* Every hero has one unique **Main Ability**.
* Main Ability can be upgraded.
* Main Ability is activated manually using its dedicated ability button.
* Some heroes share the same Main Ability.

## Healing Ability

* Every hero has one **Healing Ability**.
* Healing Ability can be upgraded.
* Healing Ability is activated manually using its dedicated ability button.
* Healing Ability type depends on the hero's class.

## Passive Ability (Slot 3)

* Every hero has one Passive Ability in Slot 3.
* Passive Ability can be upgraded.
* Passive effects may always remain active or activate after:

  * Receiving damage
  * Dealing damage
  * Eliminating an enemy

## Passive Ability (Slot 4)

* Every hero has one Passive Ability in Slot 4.
* Passive Ability can be upgraded.
* Passive effects may always remain active or activate after:

  * Receiving damage
  * Dealing damage
  * Eliminating an enemy

---

# Weapons

Every hero possesses two weapons.

## Secondary Weapon

* Every hero starts with a secondary weapon.
* Examples include pistols, revolvers and sawed-off shotguns.
* Secondary weapon has unlimited ammunition.

## Primary Weapon

* Every hero has one unique Primary Weapon.
* At the beginning of Battle Royale style modes, heroes initially use their Secondary Weapon.
* After picking up ammunition, heroes automatically switch to their Primary Weapon.
* When Primary Weapon ammunition is exhausted, heroes automatically switch back to their Secondary Weapon until more ammunition is collected.

---

# Weapon Modification

Heroes can modify and tune their Primary Weapon using weapon modifications.

This system becomes relevant mainly during later stages of account progression.

---

# Gear System

Each hero has:

* 6 Self Gear slots
* 3 Team Gear slots

Equipping gear provides various stat bonuses.

The gear system becomes important primarily during mid and late account progression.

---

# Automatic Firing

Heroes fire automatically whenever an enemy enters the weapon's red firing cone.

Players only control:

* Movement
* Aim direction
* Ability usage

---

# Movement Noise

Heroes generate movement noise.

Enemy players can detect movement through visible red footsteps, even when separated by walls or other obstacles.

Movement noise depends on:

* Movement speed
* Hero
* Hero class

Generally:

* Slow movement produces a smaller noise radius.
* Fast movement produces a larger noise radius.

Hearing range also varies between heroes.

---

# Ammunition Rules

The following game modes require players to collect ammunition before using their Primary Weapon:

* Battle Royale
* King of the Hill
* Arcade (mode dependent)

The following game modes provide unlimited Primary Weapon ammunition from the beginning:

* Squad vs Squad
* Sabotage
* Team Death Match

---

# Camera Perspective

Bullet Echo uses a **top-down camera perspective**.

Players view the battlefield from above and directly control:

- Hero movement
- Aim direction
- Ability usage

Heroes automatically fire when an enemy enters their weapon's red firing cone.

---

# Map Pickups

During a match, heroes can collect various pickups scattered around the map.

Available pickups include:

- Ammunition
- Upgrades
- Armor Charges
- Healing Ability Charges
- Main Ability Charges

---

# Charges

## Armor Charge

- Restores armor to maximum.

---

## Healing Ability Charge

- Restores one Healing Ability charge.
- Cannot exceed the hero's maximum Healing Ability charge capacity.

---

## Main Ability Charge

- Restores one Main Ability charge.
- Cannot exceed the hero's maximum Main Ability charge capacity.

---

# Upgrades

Upgrades provide temporary stat bonuses for the current match.

Upgrade rarity:

- Green — Lowest bonus
- Blue — Medium bonus
- Purple — Highest bonus

| Upgrade | Effect | Available To |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| Scope | Increases red firing cone length | Troopers, Snipers |
| Visor / Goggles | Increases vision range | Scouts, Ambushers, Snipers |
| Hand Grip / Stability Grip | Reduces bullet spread and recoil | Tanks, Troopers, scouts |
| Silencer | Reduces weapon sound. Prevents hero name and weapon icon from appearing in broadcast after eliminating an enemy. | Troopers, Snipers |
| Extended Magazine | Increases magazine size | All heroes |
| Armor Enhancer | Increases maximum armor | Tanks, Ambushers, Troopers |
| Exploding Ammo | Increases bullet damage | All heroes |

# Game Modes

## Battle Royale

* 3-player teams
* Total 5 teams
* Safe zone continuously shrinks
* Last surviving team wins

---

## King of the Hill

* Solo players
* Total 12 players
* Safe zone continuously shrinks
* Last surviving player wins

---

## Squad vs Squad

* 5-player teams
* Total 2 teams
* Safe zone continuously shrinks
* Last surviving team wins

---

## Sabotage

* 5-player teams
* Total 2 teams
* Best of 3 rounds
* Two bomb sites
* Attackers plant the bomb
* Defenders defuse the bomb
* Similar objective to Counter-Strike
* Hero used in previous round cannot be selected in the next round
* Revives are almost impossible
* No shrinking circle

---

## Team Death Match

* 3-player teams
* Total 5 teams
* Circle shrinks in multiple stages
* Hero used in previous round cannot be selected in the next round
* Revives are almost impossible

---

## Arcade

* Rules vary depending on the currently available Arcade mode.

# Hero Factions

Every hero also belongs to one faction.

Factions primarily represent lore, visual style and certain hero characteristics.

Current factions are:

* Skytech Megacorp
* Renegades
* Force and Arms
* P.Y.R.O.

---

# Classes vs Factions

Hero Class and Hero Faction are independent classifications.

Hero Class mainly determines:

* Weapon category
* Healing Ability type
* Certain gameplay characteristics

Hero Faction mainly determines:

* Lore
* Visual theme
* Shared faction identity
* Some gameplay characteristics
