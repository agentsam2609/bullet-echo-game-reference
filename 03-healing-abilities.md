# Healing Abilities

Every hero has one **Healing Ability**.

There are five types of Healing Abilities:

| Healing Ability | Hero Class               |
| --------------- | ------------------------ |
| Battle Kit      | Tanks (Gunners)          |
| Team Healing    | Troopers (Assault Rifle) |
| Team Recovery   | Ambushers (Enforcers)    |
| Stim            | Scouts (SMG)             |
| Bandage         | Snipers                  |

Numerical values such as healing amount, duration, cooldown, movement speed bonus and temporary buffs may vary depending on the ability level.

---

## Battle Kit

**Description**

* Used by all Tanks (Gunners).
* Takes **5 seconds** to apply.
* Hero cannot shoot while applying.
* Movement speed reduced by **90%** while applying.
* Upon completion:

  * Restores Health (depends on ability level).
  * Restores Armor (depends on ability level).
* After being upgraded:

  * Temporarily increases maximum Health.
  * Temporarily increases maximum Armor.

---

## Team Healing

**Description**

* Used by all Troopers (Assault Rifle heroes).
* Lasts **5–9 seconds** (depends on ability level).
* Restores Health every second to the hero and nearby allies.
* Healing radius depends on ability level.
* Hero can shoot while healing.
* Hero's movement speed is reduced by **50%** during the effect.
* After being upgraded:

  * Reduces incoming damage for the hero and nearby allies.

---

## Team Recovery

**Description**

* Used by all Ambushers (Enforcers).
* Lasts **4–8 seconds** (depends on ability level).
* Restores Health and Armor every second to the hero and nearby allies.
* Healing radius depends on ability level.
* Hero cannot shoot while the ability is active.
* No movement speed reduction.
* After being upgraded:

  * When the ability finishes naturally or is interrupted, additional Health and Armor are restored.

---

## Stim

**Description**

* Used by all Scouts (SMG heroes).
* Takes **0.5 seconds** to apply.
* Hero cannot shoot while applying.
* Upon completion:

  * Restores Health every second for **4–8 seconds** (depends on ability level).
* After being upgraded:

  * Grants a temporary movement speed bonus during the healing duration.

---

## Bandage

**Description**

* Used by all Snipers.
* Applies instantly (with animation).
* Restores Health (depends on ability level).
* After being upgraded:

  * Temporarily reduces the hero's movement noise (depends on ability level).

---

# Healing Ability Charges

* Healing Abilities consume one Healing Ability charge when activated.
* Healing Ability Charges can be collected from the map.
* Each pickup restores **one** Healing Ability charge.
* Charges cannot exceed the hero's maximum Healing Ability charge capacity.

---

# Notes

* Every hero has exactly one Healing Ability.
* Healing Ability type is determined by the hero's class.
* Main Ability Charges and Healing Ability Charges are independent.
* Health, Armor, duration, radius, movement speed modifiers and other numerical values depend on the ability level.
