# Main Abilities

Every hero has one unique **Main Ability**, which gives each hero a distinct playstyle. Some heroes share the same Main Ability.

Numerical values such as damage, duration, radius, cooldown, movement speed, piercing power and other statistics may vary depending on the ability level.

---

## Shield

**Description**

* Temporary portable shield that the hero holds.
* The shield absorbs all incoming bullet damage.
* Hero and allies can pass through the shield.
* Bullets fired by the hero and allies pass through the shield.
* Enemy bullets cannot pass through the shield.

---

## Force Field

**Description**

* Temporary stationary shield placed on the map.
* The shield absorbs all incoming bullet damage.
* Hero and allies can freely pass through the shield.
* Bullets fired by the hero and allies pass through the shield.
* Enemies and enemy bullets cannot pass through the shield.

---

## Invisibility

**Description**

* Temporary invisibility.
* Slightly increases movement speed.
* Hero cannot fire until the ability ends.
* Footsteps remain visible to enemies.

---

## Grenade

**Description**

* Throws a grenade.
* Explodes after a short delay.
* Deals damage within its explosion radius to all heroes, including allies.
* Obstacles block explosion damage.

---

## Grenade Launcher / Rocket

**Description**

* Fires a rocket projectile.
* Travels a long distance.
* Explodes only upon impact.
* Deals damage within its explosion radius to all heroes, including allies.
* Obstacles block explosion damage.

---

## Turret

**Description**

* Places a stationary turret.
* Fires automatically when an enemy enters its red firing cone.
* Turret has health and can be destroyed.
* Turret has a frontal shield that blocks bullets from the front.
* Expires after a duration (depends on ability level).

---

## Breakaway

**Description**

* Hero jumps backwards.
* Ignores walls, obstacles and enemies while jumping.
* Jump distance depends on ability level.
* After landing:

  * Movement speed is temporarily increased.
  * Firing range is temporarily increased.

---

## Blink

**Description**

* Hero jumps forward.
* Ignores walls, obstacles and enemies while jumping.
* Jump distance depends on ability level.
* Upon landing, deals damage within a radius (depends on ability level).

---

## Scan

**Description**

* Reveals enemy locations within a radius (depends on ability level).
* Reveal duration depends on ability level.
* Revealed enemies receive bonus damage.

---

## Thermal Vision

**Description**

* Hero moves slower while the ability is active.
* Hero can shoot through walls during the active duration.
* Increases bullet piercing power (depends on ability level).
* Eliminating first enemy grants +1 Main Ability charge.

---

## Suppression

**Description**

* Hero stops moving and continuously fires in the chosen direction until ammunition is exhausted.
* Increases bullet piercing power (depends on ability level).
* Increases vision range (depends on ability level).
* Increases reload speed (depends on ability level).
* Movement speed is reduced by 95% (depends on ability level).

---

## Ball Lightning

**Description**

* Launches a ball of lightning that slowly moves forward.
* Passes through obstacles.
* Cancels any Main Ability it touches.
* Reduces damage taken by the hero and allies inside the ball (depends on ability level).
* Ball remains active for a duration (depends on ability level).
* If two Ball Lightning abilities collide:

  * Both explode.
  * Deal damage (depends on ability level).
  * Remove all Main Ability charges of heroes within the affected area.

---

## Fire Hunter

**Description**

* Launches a hunter projectile.
* Moves forward like a grenade.
* Activates after a delay or upon contact with a hero or ability.
* Expands rapidly and creates an ignition zone.

Inside the ignition zone:

* Movement speed is reduced (depends on ability level).
* Initial contact deals damage (depends on ability level).
* Continuous damage is applied every second.
* Zone duration depends on ability level.

---

## Portal Gun

**Description**

* Fires portal darts that attach to walls and other surfaces.
* A portal is created after the second dart lands. allies and enemies can travel from both ends.
* If both darts land at the same location, a tunnel is created with an energy burst at the second portal end.

Energy burst effects:

* Reduces movement speed (depends on ability level).
* Reduces fire rate (depends on ability level).
* Effect duration depends on ability level.
* Effect radius depends on ability level.

Additional mechanics:

* Darts remain active for 45 seconds.
* Existing portals can be cancelled if another hero places a dart nearby or outside zone.
* Hero(only self) passing through a portal receive:

  * Movement speed bonus.
  * Chance to evade enemy bullets (depends on ability level).

---

## Rain of Arrows

**Description**

* Hero selects a target area.
* A rain of arrows falls within the selected radius (depends on ability level).
* Deals damage to all heroes inside the area (depends on ability level).

---

## Force Bomb

**Description**

* Launches a Force Bomb.
* Explodes upon collision.

Explosion effects:

* Deals damage (depends on ability level).
* Blast radius depends on ability level.
* Knocks back all heroes.
* Stuns affected heroes.
* Applies burning damage every second for a duration (depends on ability level).

While stunned:

* Abilities cannot be used.
* Movement speed is reduced.

Friendly heroes receive approximately 70% reduced stun duration.

---

## Shadow Step

**Description**

* Hero dashes forward in the facing direction.
* Dash distance depends on ability level.
* Hero is immune to almost all enemy damage during the dash.
* Dash passes through:

  * Enemies
  * Narrow passages
  * Force Fields
* Passing through an enemy:

  * Deals damage (depends on ability level).
  * Increases movement speed for a duration (depends on ability level).

---

## Rampage

**Description**

* Hero enters Rampage state.
* Runs forward with increased movement speed (depends on ability level).
* Gains a high-health barrier (depends on ability level).

Upon colliding with an enemy:

* Knocks back nearby heroes.
* Deals damage (depends on ability level).
* Applies stun (depends on ability level).

While stunned:

* Cannot shoot.
* Cannot use abilities.
* Movement speed is reduced.

Rampage ends when:

* Barrier is destroyed.
* Hero collides with an enemy.
* Rampage time ends.

---

## Freezing Mine

**Description**

* Plants a mine.
* Mine remains invisible until enemies move very close.
* Upon detonation:

  * Freezes all heroes within the explosion radius.

Frozen heroes:

* Have reduced movement speed (depends on ability level).
* Can not shoot.
* Can not use abilities.
* Receive Frozen HP (depends on ability level) for a duration.

While frozen:

* Any hit destroys all Frozen HP before applying leftover dmg to HP.

---

## Burrow

**Description**

* Hero moves underground for a duration (depends on ability level).
* Vision is disabled while underground.
* Continuously scans nearby enemies above ground in close radius (depends on ability level).

When Burrow ends:

* Hero jumps back to the surface.
* Deals damage (depends on ability level).
* Disarms nearby enemies for a duration (depends on ability level).

If interrupted by explosions or Blink abilities:

* Burrow ends immediately.
* Disarm effect is not applied.

---

## Hook

**Description**

* Launches a hook in a straight line.
* Hook range depends on ability level.

The hook can attach to:

* Heroes
* Grenades
* Turrets
* Rockets
* Fire Hunters
* Force Bombs
* Crates

Effects:

* Pulled enemies receive damage (depends on ability level).
* Pulled enemies receive Reduced movement speed.
* Pulled enemies receive Reduced maximum health.
* Pulled enemies receive Reduced fire rate.
* Debuff duration depends on ability level.

Additional mechanics:

* Pulled crates automatically open upon reaching the hero.
* Hooking a wall or object pulls the hero toward it.
* Knocked-down heroes can also be pulled near hero.

---

## Gravity Grenade

**Description**

* Throws a Gravity Grenade.
* Explodes upon impact.
* Deals damage (depends on ability level).

Creates a gravity field:

* Radius depends on ability level.
* Duration depends on ability level.
* Pulls heroes, abilities and pickups toward its center.

Enemies inside the gravity field:

* Receive increased damage.
* Have increased bullet spread.

Friendly heroes receive approximately 50% reduced negative effects.

---

## Throwing Shield

**Description**

* Throws a shield forward for a duration (depends on ability level).
* Shield ricochets off obstacles up to a maximum number of times (depends on ability level).
* Returns to the hero afterward.
* Blocks all bullets while in flight.

Additional mechanics:

* Can catch enemy projectiles such as grenades, rockets, Force Bombs and Fire Hunters.
* Captured projectiles explode upon the shield's next collision.
* Hitting an enemy:

  * Deals damage to enemy (depends on ability level).
  * Reduces fire rate of enemy (depends on ability level).
* Can chain to nearby uncovered enemies.
* Hero gains movement speed for each ricochet of enemy hit (depends on ability level).
* While carried on the hero's back, reduces rear damage by 50%.

---

## Decoy

**Description**

* Hero becomes invisible for a duration (depends on ability level).
* Spawns a decoy with 50% Health and Armor.
* Decoy moves forward and targets the nearest enemy.
* Decoy deals no damage.

When destroyed:

* Explodes.
* Blinds enemies within an area (depends on ability level).
* Increases damage taken.
* Reduces vision.
* Effect duration depends on ability level.

Once per battle:

* Upon reaching 0 Health:

  * Hero survives with 1 Health.
  * All Main Ability charges are lost.
  * Hero becomes invisible.
  * A downed decoy is left behind.
  * Decoy explodes after a delay with the same blind effect.

After invisibility ends:

* Hero cannot shoot for 0.3 seconds.

---

## Toxic Hounds

**Description**

* Summons two hounds.
* Hounds can spit toxin or explode near enemies.
* Toxin creates an area of effect.

Enemies touching the toxin area:

* Become poisoned.
* Lose Health every second.
* Have reduced movement speed.
* Poison remains active for a duration after leaving the area.

Additional mechanics:

* Hero and summoned hounds are immune to toxin effects.
* Hounds can be ordered to:

  * Follow the hero.
  * Guard a selected area.
* While the ability is active:

  * One fallen hero can be summoned with approximately 20% of their original main stats.
  * The summoned hero follows hero and attacks nearby enemies.
* Ability duration is 30 seconds.
